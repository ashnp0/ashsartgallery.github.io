About the Artist

I am Ashley Nicole Permelona, a student at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology. I am an artist who is still in the early stages of my artistic journey. I must confess that I rarely find myself drawing, only doing so when the mood strikes me. Additionally, I have yet to settle on my own art style as I am still in the process of exploration. However, despite these uncertainties, my passion for art remains undeterred, and I eagerly embrace the process of self-discovery as I seek to find my artistic identity.

About Ash's Art Gallery

I have always been drawn to simplicity and its ability to communicate profound messages with clarity and elegance. In a world filled with constant noise and complexity, I find solace and beauty in the simplicity of forms and lines. By stripping away the excess, my digital artworks create a sense of tranquility that invites viewers to pause and reconnect with their own inner world. Through my minimalistic approach, I aim to engage viewers in a dialogue between the artwork and their own perceptions. Simple lines and shapes become a visual language, open to interpretation and personal connection. It is my hope that each viewer can find their own unique meaning and resonance within my art.
